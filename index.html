Fintech

What is an Irish Fintech Company anyway?

Glad you asked. Let's define what it's NOT.

A company founded outside of Ireland, even if they have an office in Ireland or the founders are born and raised in Ireland.

A company that is “Coming Soon”, in “beta” – or is no longer operating.

A company that services several industries or sectors, even if Financial Services is part of your market.

A company that provides consulting, managed services or people-based solutions (Excel doesn't count!)

FintechIRELAND

Leading the promotion, support and facilitation of the Ireland's Fintech ecosystem.

Peter Oakes formally founded Fintech Ireland in August 2014 to give a unique identity to the promotion and facilitation of the fintech scene in Ireland and promotes the Irish fintech scene globally. I joined as a collaborator in 2015 to add my support to this excellent nonprofit initiative.

I also maintain @FintechBridge and @FintechBattle
