But first, some clarifications. What is a "Fintech company" anyway? Glad you asked. Let's define what it's not

Your company isn’t Fintech Ireland if it’s:

  • A company founded outside of Ireland, even if they have an office in Ireland or the founders are born and raised in Ireland. Sorry Stripe.
  • A company that services several industries or sectors, even if Financial Services is part of your market. Sorry Intercom.
  • A company that is “Coming Soon”, in “beta” – or is no longer operating. Sorry Anglo Irish Bank.
  • A company that provides consulting, managed services or people-based solutions – even if they use Excel, it’s not Fintech. Sorry Accenture Tech Labs.
